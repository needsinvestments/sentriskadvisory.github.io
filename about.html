About

Sent Risk Advisory was established in 2021 with the objective of building a structured and credible insurance advisory platform aligned with evolving business risks and economic realities.

The firm is driven by professionally qualified individuals, including engineers and corporate professionals with over 16+ years of experience across IT and ITES sectors. This background brings a disciplined, analytical and systems-oriented approach to understanding risk and designing insurance solutions.

Context & Perspective

India’s growing global presence across industries has led to increasing complexity in operational, financial and liability-related risks. As businesses scale, the nature of exposure evolves, requiring a more structured and informed approach to risk management.

At the same time, the insurance sector in India is undergoing significant expansion, creating both opportunities and responsibilities for professionals within the ecosystem. There is a growing need for advisory-led engagement rather than product-driven interactions.

Our Approach

Sent Risk Advisory approaches insurance as a decision-making framework rather than a transactional requirement. The focus remains on aligning coverage with actual exposure, improving clarity in decision-making and ensuring continuity in protection.

By integrating risk understanding, insurance structuring and insurer engagement, the firm aims to provide solutions that are both technically sound and practically relevant.

Our Direction

The long-term objective is to contribute meaningfully to the growth of India’s insurance ecosystem by promoting a more structured, responsible and advisory-driven approach to risk.

Sent Risk Advisory aims to position itself as a trusted partner for businesses and individuals seeking clarity, reliability and long-term alignment in their insurance decisions.
