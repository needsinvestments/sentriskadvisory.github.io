About Sent Risk Advisory

Sent Risk Advisory was established in 2021 under Sathya Axis Enterprises with a clear intent — to build a disciplined and credible insurance advisory platform focused on understanding and structuring risk in a meaningful way.

The firm is led by professionally qualified individuals, including engineers and experienced professionals with over 16 years of corporate exposure across IT and ITES sectors. This background brings a structured, analytical and process-driven approach to insurance advisory, distinguishing it from conventional transactional practices in the industry.

Our transition into the insurance domain is driven by a strong belief that risk management and insurance will play a critical role in the growth story of 21st century India. As businesses scale, diversify and integrate globally, the need for structured risk coverage becomes increasingly important.

India’s growing global presence across industries — including technology, manufacturing, services and infrastructure — is creating new opportunities as well as new forms of risk. In this evolving landscape, insurance is no longer a compliance requirement, but a strategic tool for continuity, resilience and long-term stability.

The insurance industry in India is still underpenetrated relative to the scale and potential of the economy. This presents a significant opportunity for professionals and advisory-driven platforms to contribute meaningfully towards building awareness, improving coverage quality and strengthening risk management practices across businesses.

At Sent Risk Advisory, our focus is not merely on placing insurance policies, but on improving the way businesses and individuals understand and approach risk. We work across insurers to design solutions that are aligned with actual exposure, operational realities and long-term protection needs.

Our ambition is to build a trusted advisory platform that combines structured thinking, professional discipline and practical execution — contributing to both client outcomes and the broader development of the insurance ecosystem in India.
