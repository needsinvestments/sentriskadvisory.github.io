Insurance Advisory & Solutions

Our services are structured around key areas of business risk, providing advisory, design and placement of insurance solutions aligned with operational realities and long-term protection needs.

Group Health Insurance (GMC / Employee Benefits)

Structuring comprehensive employee health programs including Group Medical Cover, Group Personal Accident and wellness-linked benefits.

Focus areas include policy design, insurer selection, coverage optimisation, cost control and ongoing support for employee-centric risk management.

Fire & Property Insurance

Coverage for physical assets including buildings, plant & machinery, stock and infrastructure against fire and allied perils.

Advisory includes correct asset valuation, risk exposure assessment and structuring policies aligned with actual operational and financial risk.

Liability Insurance

Protection against legal liabilities arising from business operations, including Public Liability, Product Liability, Professional Indemnity and Directors & Officers (D&O).

Focus on identifying exposure areas, regulatory implications and structuring coverage to mitigate financial and reputational risks.

Marine Insurance

Coverage for goods in transit, both domestic and international, including marine cargo and logistics-related exposures.

Structuring policies based on supply chain, transit modes and frequency of movement, ensuring continuity of coverage across operations.

Engineering Insurance

Coverage for projects, machinery and infrastructure including Contractor’s All Risk (CAR), Erection All Risk (EAR) and Machinery Breakdown policies.

Advisory includes project-specific risk evaluation, contract requirements and alignment with execution timelines and asset lifecycle.

Customised Insurance Programs

Tailored insurance structures for SMEs and evolving businesses requiring flexible, cost-effective and scalable coverage solutions.

Focus on balancing adequate protection with financial efficiency and operational needs.
