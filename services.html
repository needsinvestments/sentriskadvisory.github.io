Insurance Advisory & Solutions

Our services are structured around key areas of risk, providing advisory, design and placement of insurance solutions aligned with operational realities, financial exposure and long-term protection needs.

Group Health Insurance (GMC / Employee Benefits)

Employee health risk is one of the most immediate and visible exposures for any organisation. Rising healthcare costs and employee expectations make structured coverage essential.

Inadequate policies can impact employee retention, productivity and financial stability. A well-designed program strengthens workforce confidence and organisational resilience.

We structure balanced employee benefit programs aligning coverage, cost and long-term sustainability.

Fire & Property Insurance

Physical assets represent significant capital investment. Events such as fire, natural calamities or operational incidents can disrupt business continuity and lead to substantial financial loss.

Incorrect valuation or inadequate coverage often results in claim shortfalls, exposing businesses to avoidable financial stress.

We ensure accurate asset valuation and structure policies aligned with actual exposure.

Liability Insurance

Businesses face increasing legal and third-party liabilities arising from operations, products, professional services and management decisions.

These risks are often underestimated until a claim occurs, by which time the financial and reputational impact can be significant.

We identify exposure areas and structure liability coverage including Public Liability, Product Liability, Professional Indemnity and D&O policies.

Marine Insurance

Movement of goods exposes businesses to transit-related risks such as damage, theft and loss across supply chains.

Gaps in transit coverage can disrupt operations and impact financial performance, especially for businesses dependent on continuous logistics.

We align policies with actual movement patterns, routes and operational cycles.

Engineering Insurance

Projects, machinery and infrastructure involve complex risks during installation and operational phases, including breakdown and project delays.

Standard policies may not adequately address project-specific exposures, leading to gaps in protection.

We structure Engineering policies such as CAR, EAR and Machinery Breakdown aligned with project scope and lifecycle.

Customised Insurance Programs (SMEs)

SMEs face diverse risks but often operate with limited resources to manage them. Standard policies may either under-cover or create unnecessary financial burden.

The challenge lies in balancing adequate protection with cost efficiency.

We design scalable, practical insurance programs aligned with business growth.

Personal Insurance & Financial Protection

Individuals face financial risks that can impact long-term security, including loss of income, medical emergencies and future obligations.

Lack of structured planning can affect family stability, retirement readiness and key life goals such as children’s education.

We provide advisory on life insurance, health cover, retirement planning and goal-based protection to ensure financial continuity and long-term security.
